Lea and Martha

share a passion for aestethic bakery. They both have a diploma in baking and decor design from Lund's Sundsvall Bageriet and as soon as they finished it, they knew that they want to own their own bakery for specialising in cakes. When they don't think about cake decor, you can usually find them hiking in the woods, lying at the beach or enjoying the atmosphere of a cozy café in town.

History

It all started when Lea and Martha had to design a theme cake together in one of their classes. They immediately became friends and spent every free minute dreaming about their cake shop. Then in 2021, they had the opportunity to host a pop-up store in front of Café Ringlan, where they could show-off their baking skills. People were so enthusiastic about their goods that they started asking when they will open their own shop. For the next year, Lea and Martha only took orders from friends while they slowly started building their concept. They knew that they wanted to focus on designing cakes rather than selling, so they installed this website, instead of renting a bakery or café. But every now and then, they are guests in other cafés and host a pop-up store with their creations.
